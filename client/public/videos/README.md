# Background Videos

Place your background video files here:
- bg1.mp4
- bg2.mp4
- bg3.mp4

## Video Specifications
- Format: MP4
- Recommended size: 1920x1080 or smaller
- Duration: 5-15 seconds (will loop)
- File size: Keep under 5MB for better performance

## Current Usage
These videos appear in the HeroModal background:
- Top left corner
- Top right corner
- Bottom left
- Bottom right
- Middle left (behind left frame)
- Middle right (behind right frame)

All videos are:
- Blurred (blur-sm or blur-md)
- Low opacity (12-20%)
- Non-interactive (pointer-events-none)
- Positioned to not collide with modal frames
